Intro

A Post-Graduate in Finance and Accounting from NMIMS with a two-year work experience.

An Ex- Risk Analyst in EY, I retraced my passions to build a career in the two fields that I am most passionate about: UI/UX Design and Bharatnatyam.

UI/UX Design

Aspiring Product Designer, currently I am completing my Mastercamp Certification in UI/UX from Masters Union.

While Risk Analytics taught me to think in a structured manner, to break down the large chunks of data, analyse it and draw meaningful insights, I also derived a deeper understanding of various business process involved in the FMCG, Pharmaceuticals and Automobile sectors.

At EY, I not only worked on data analytics, but also created powerful visual dashboards on PowerBi, Tableau and Qlliksense. Working with the design team at EY helped me understand where my interest lies.

To convert my passions into a career, I am currently pursuing the Mastercamp Certification in User Experience Design from Masters Union, Delhi. I am also pursuing an MA degree in Psychology from IGNOU.
